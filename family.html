For Family & Friends

HELP THEM
BE HEARD

If someone you love is incarcerated and has a story to tell, you can set up and run their page from the outside. Here's how.

How it works

THE PROCESS

01
FILL OUT THIS FORM
Give us your loved one's name, ID, facility, and your contact info. Tell us you want to be their page manager.
02
WE MAIL THEM
We send a release form directly to your loved one inside. They sign it naming you as their outside manager and mail it back to us.
03
YOU'RE AUTHORIZED
Once we receive the signed form, you're set up as their manager. You get access to upload content and manage their page.
04
START PUBLISHING
They mail you their writing and artwork. You scan it and upload it to their page. Their readers get notified automatically.
What you can do

YOUR ROLE

📄
Upload Content
Scan their handwritten letters and artwork and upload them directly to their page. Phone camera works fine, flatbed scanner is better.
🛍
Manage Their Store
Set prices for their work, manage commissions, and handle the financial side of their page on their behalf.
💳
Handle Payments
Pay their $10/month paid tier by card or PayPal. Receive their 80% sales share and forward it to them via money order to their institution.
📬
Stay Connected
Get email notifications when readers comment or purchase. Be the bridge between your loved one and the people reading their work.
Request access

SET UP A PAGE

Fill this out and we'll mail a release form to your loved one within the week. Once it comes back signed, their page goes live and you're in control.
