Bio / Story

My name is Nicolas Losito. Inside, they call me Redbeard the Great — though the alias has had other forms. It started as a joke. I was teaching myself card tricks in my cell in late 2020, and I thought: every good magician needs a name. Redbeard the Great. Coincidence? I think not.

"Out in the real world, I'm a junkie fuckup. In here, apparently I'm one of the smart ones. People come to me for wisdom and insight and fun facts."
— 11/23/20

Starting in 2017, I wrote letters home from inside NCDAC. Those letters became a memoir — Razor Wire Feels — 52 episodes across 5 parts. When they gave us tablets in 2021, the letters became texts. The format changed; the voice didn't. Each episode ends the same: "This has been another episode of Razor Wire Feels, I'm your host, Redbeard the [fill in the blank]."

During the pandemic I used my COVID stimulus check to draw K-Master K & The Hammer: Issue #0, 26 pages. In 2022 I founded Bich Fist Comics (yes, spelled exactly like that — in reference to BC Rich Bich guitars) as the publishing arm. I also finished a short story, The Ravaged, 17 handwritten pages, second draft final. And I have a son, Mason, who at age 6 saw a picture of me for the first time and told his mom — proudly — "My Dad comes home from prison next year."

This page is mine. It is also the template for everyone inside who deserves one.
