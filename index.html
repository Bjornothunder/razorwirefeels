There are approximately 32,000 people incarcerated in North Carolina state prisons right now. Most of them will never be heard. Not because they have nothing to say — but because no one built them a place to say it.

Razor Wire Feels is that place. A permanent public page for every incarcerated person in NC who wants one. Their writing. Their art. Their story. Published, preserved, and open to the world.

Every page on this platform was created by someone on the inside. Every word was written behind a wall. We just made sure it got out.
