Before You Submit — Read This
YO. THE POLICE ARE GONNA READ THIS.

This platform is public. That means the entire internet can see it — including correctional officers, wardens, prosecutors, parole boards, and anyone else with a browser. I'm not going to sugarcoat that.

I strongly suggest you keep what you post clean and legal. Don't give anybody new material to work with. This is meant to be your voice, not another case on your hands. This shit is out for the whole world to see. Top flight.

That said — you have every right to speak your truth, write your story, share your art, and be heard. That's exactly what this platform is for. Just be smart about it.

🚫
ONE HARD RULE. NON-NEGOTIABLE.
This platform will not publish content from anyone convicted of sex offenses against children or women — and will not publish any content of a sexual nature involving minors or non-consenting individuals. Period. No exceptions. No appeals.

This is my corner of the internet. I built it. I set the rules. If that applies to you — go find your own corner. Every other offense, every other story, every other voice is welcome here. Not that one.

Common Questions

FAQ

Do I keep the rights to my writing and artwork? ›
Yes. 100%. You own everything you create. Razor Wire Feels publishes on your behalf — we don't claim ownership of anything. If a publisher comes calling about something you posted here, that deal is yours.
Can I use an alias instead of my real name? ›
Yes. Your page can display your alias as your main name. Your legal name and ID are used for our internal records only. Write under whatever name you choose.
What if I get transferred to another facility? ›
Your page stays exactly where it is. Just write us a letter from your new facility letting us know your new address. Nothing changes on your page.
Is this connected to NCDAC in any way? ›
No. Razor Wire Feels has no affiliation with NCDAC, the state of North Carolina, or any correctional facility. We are an independent platform. We do not share participant information with correctional institutions.
How do I get paid if someone buys my work? ›
Your 80% cut is sent via money order to your institution or to a designated outside person. We process payouts monthly for balances of $10 or more. Under $10 rolls to the following month.
What kind of content can I post? ›
Anything you create — memoir, fiction, poetry, essays, letters, comedy, philosophy, artwork, comics. Strong language, dark themes, and political views are all fine. The one hard rule: no sexual content involving minors or non-consenting adults, and no pages for those convicted of sex offenses against children or women. That's the only line.
